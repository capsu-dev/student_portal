Install CAPSU Student Portal
Important
The CAPSU Student Portal is currently distributed as an APK while awaiting publication on the Google Play Store. During installation, Android may display a security warning because the application was downloaded outside Google Play.
Installation Steps
1 Download the APK

Tap the Download APK button below and wait for the download to finish.

2 Open the APK

Open the downloaded APK from your browser or Downloads folder.

Android may display one of the following messages:
  • For your security, your phone is not allowed to install unknown apps from this source.
  • Blocked by Play Protect.
3 Allow Installation

If Android blocks the installation:

  1. Tap Settings.
  2. Enable Allow from this source or Allow app installs.
  3. Return to the installer.

If Google Play Protect appears:

  1. Tap More details (if available).
  2. Select Install anyway.
4 Install
  1. Tap Install.
  2. Wait until installation completes.
  3. Tap Open.
5 Sign In

Log in using your official CAPSU student account to access university services.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why does Android warn that the app may be harmful?
Android automatically warns users when installing applications outside Google Play. This is expected and does not necessarily indicate that the application is unsafe.

Is the app available on Google Play?
Not yet. The application is currently undergoing the publication process.

Need assistance?
For technical concerns, please contact the Capiz State University Information and Communication Technology Office through the University's official communication channels.
