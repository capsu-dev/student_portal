Privacy Policy for CAPSU Student Portal

Effective Date: July 18, 2026

Privacy Policy

Capiz State University ("CAPSU") values the privacy of its students and is committed to protecting personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the CAPSU Student Portal mobile application.

The CAPSU Student Portal is intended to provide students with secure access to university services such as viewing personal information, enrollment status, class schedules, grades, payment records, academic calendar, university announcements, and other student-related services.

By using the application, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

Information We Access

The application retrieves information associated with your official CAPSU student account after successful authentication. Depending on the services you access, the application may display:

  • Student ID number
  • Full name
  • Student photograph
  • Program, campus, year level, and section
  • Enrollment information
  • Class schedules
  • Academic grades
  • Student ledger and payment records
  • University announcements and academic calendar

The application also uses authentication credentials and access tokens issued by the official CAPSU server to verify your identity and maintain a secure session.

The application does not permanently store your password.

How Information Is Used

The information retrieved through the application is used only for purposes related to university services. Specifically, it is used to:

  • Verify your identity during login.
  • Display your personal and academic information.
  • Allow access to your class schedule, grades, enrollment status, and payment records.
  • Display university announcements and academic events.
  • Maintain secure user sessions while the application is in use.
  • Improve the reliability and security of the application.

The application does not use your personal information for advertising, marketing, or profiling.

Local Storage

To improve usability and maintain secure access, the application stores limited information on your device, including:

  • Authentication tokens
  • Session information
  • Temporary application preferences

This information is stored only for application functionality.

Passwords are never stored on the device after successful authentication.

Data Sharing

The CAPSU Student Portal does not sell, rent, or disclose student information to third parties.

All academic and student information displayed by the application is obtained from authorized Capiz State University information systems and is accessible only after successful user authentication.

Information may only be disclosed when required by applicable laws or university policies.

Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are implemented to protect student information.

These measures include secure authentication, encrypted communication with university servers through HTTPS whenever available, automatic session expiration after periods of inactivity, and secure handling of locally stored authentication tokens.

Although every reasonable effort is made to safeguard information, no method of electronic storage or internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Session Management

For security purposes, the application automatically ends inactive sessions after a specified period. Authentication data stored on the device may also be removed after an extended period of inactivity, requiring users to sign in again.

Users are encouraged to log out after using shared or public devices.

User Responsibilities

Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their login credentials and for ensuring that their devices are adequately protected against unauthorized access.

Any suspected unauthorized use of a student account should be reported immediately to the appropriate university office.

Third-Party Components

The application may include standard Android system components and open-source software libraries necessary for its operation. These components are used solely to support the application's functionality and are not intended to collect student information beyond what is required for normal operation.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in university services, legal requirements, or application functionality.

Any revisions will be published together with the updated effective date. Continued use of the application after such changes constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
